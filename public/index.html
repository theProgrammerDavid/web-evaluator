Web Evaluator

Proof of concept — self-hosted on a spare computer at home. Each job takes approximately ~10 seconds to complete.
Analysis is powered by GPT-4o. AI ratings are a rough signal — treat them as a starting point, not gospel.
Results are public. Anyone with a job link can view its screenshots and analysis. Do not submit confidential, private, or password-protected URLs. As this is a demo, submissions are rate limited to 5 per IP per minute.

Heatmap mode is best suited for static pages. It is not designed for websites with dynamic or moving components (carousels, animations, live data), as these will produce noisy diffs that don't reflect real layout changes. If your site has animations, the crawler will likely capture them at different stages between runs, generating a heatmap that looks like something changed when it didn't . But this false positive is actually useful as it gives you a realistic preview of what the tool would look like detecting a genuine layout regression in a production setting.
